The Foundation

The Problem

Deals don't die from a bad pitch.

They die from follow-ups that quietly slip. Contacts live in one place, notes in another, and the pipeline only exists in your head — so the next thing that needs attention is easy to miss.

The Solution

One clear view of every relationship.

Track contacts, log every interaction against them, and see exactly where each deal stands in the pipeline — lightweight and fast, never an enterprise maze. The product makes "what needs my attention next" obvious, so nothing leaks.
